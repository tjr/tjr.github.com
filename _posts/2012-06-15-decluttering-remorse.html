Last year, in the face of repairing my water-damaged basement office area, I went on a bit of a decluttering rampage. While in some cases I had to painfully severe illogical emotional ties to various inanimate objects, on the whole I was happy with the results, and really haven’t missed anything that I cleared out.

Until this week, when I got to wondering, now where did I put my computer science journals? I know I recycled the magazines, but I kept the journals, didn’t I? Oops, I guess not. Yeah, I kind of wish I had kept those…
