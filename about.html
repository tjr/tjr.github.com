About Me

As a software engineer, I have worked mostly with avionics systems and web applications. I am the primary author and maintainer of The GNU C Reference Manual for the Free Software Foundation. I received a bachelor's degree in computer science from Cornell College.

As a musician, I have served my church as a player and arranger of music since 2001. In 2007 my father and I co-founded a music production company. Over the years, I've done a bit of soundtrack composing, jazz performing, and accordion playing.

I also enjoy photography, teaching, history, fine carbonated waters, and advocating for privacy rights & intellectual freedom principles.

You can email me at tjr@acm.org. If you use OpenPGP-based email encryption, feel free to encrypt mail sent to me using my public key.

About the Site Design

This web page looks not unlike something you might have seen in 1995. I've tried numerous alternate designs for this site, including plain HTML/CSS themes, Wordpress+Wordpress themes, and paid for some sort of Adobe Photoshop-generated graphical layout that looked rather neat but was absurdly cumbersome to actually use. In the end, I personally like this classic 1995-esque look, so I leave it at that.
