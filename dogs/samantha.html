Samantha

Home : Border Collie adopted in May 2011

First Meeting

Samantha was born on a farm in Vinton, Iowa. She was the only red-and-white puppy amongst eight black-and-white siblings.

Moving In

She moved in with us when she was six weeks old. At first she was a little apprehensive about her new surroundings, but it wasn't long before she was getting used to things!

Getting Bigger

Samantha didn't stay a tiny baby puppy forever! She even ventured out for a shopping trip...
