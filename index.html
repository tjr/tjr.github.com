Trevis Rothwell's home page

Welcome to my home page on the World Wide Web!

As a software engineer, I aim to use the tools and techniques of computer science to solve problems faced by real people. I'm currently staying busy at work on avionics simulation software and mobile applications.

A professional software engineer should not only write programs, but help to teach others. One of my contributions to teaching is The GNU C Reference Manual, which I maintain as a volunteer for FSF Project GNU. I have also taught at the area community college, done some independent tutoring in Lisp programming, and spoken for the local section of the IEEE.

As a musician, I have served my church as a player and arranger of music since 2001. In 2007, my father and I co-founded a music production company. Over the years, I've done a bit of soundtrack composing, jazz performing, and accordion playing.

What's New?

I copied many of my online photographs from the Google Picasaweb service to Flickr. I have found the Flickr service to have a nicer interface. I have been uploading the photos afresh from my original archives, giving opportunity for higher-resolution images in some cases, and also doing a bit of touchup on color balance and such. I do not necessarily plan to re-upload every photo that I had in Picasaweb, but if you notice any missing that you would like to see added, please let me know.
