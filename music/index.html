Music

Church

Most of musical activity involves playing and arranging music for my local church, and occasionally for traveling ministry events.

Atmosphere Control Productions

I co-founded this music production company, primarily as an outlet for creating and releasing musical recordings. Projects completed include:

  • Believing Side of Life, featuring tracks with Abraham Laboriel, Bill Maxwell, and Justo Almario.
  • Flow, an improvised keyboard duet with myself and Tom Brooks.
  • Walking in the Light, a solo keyboard recording that has been used as pre-service music at Dr. Billye Brim's annual prayer conference in Branson.
