Home : Photography

I began getting seriously interested in photography in 2005 while taking pictures for an online community website project. I read articles and discussions at photo.net voraciously, bought SLR camera equipment like the wind, and made a point of taking a batch of photos every week or two. After a couple of years, I became bored with photographing what was around my home, and now mostly only take pictures when traveling.
