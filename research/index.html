Artificial Intelligence

I first became interested in programming computers after playing some Infocom text adventure games and realizing that, with enough effort, I could make similar games myself. This prompted an interest in artificial intelligence, especially natural language processing. I've been formulating ideas for advances in natural language processing for years, but am just starting to write some things down.
