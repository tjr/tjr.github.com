Music Measurement

Home : Software : one software description

Music Measurement is a program for the Apple iPhone. It's a small specialized calculator for figuring the duration of a piece of music given the tempo and number of measures, or the tempo given the number of measures and duration, or the number of measures given the tempo and duration. It should be useful for film and radio composers, or anyone who needs to arrange a piece of music to match a particular time.
